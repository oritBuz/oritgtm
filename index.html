Work With Me

My marketing career started at large agencies — McCann Group and Universal McCann — where I developed discipline around audience strategy, media planning, and measurable campaigns at scale. From there I moved into Ananey Paramount, building brand and digital capabilities in a high-growth media environment.

For the past several years I've worked exclusively in B2B tech, working with growth-stage startups as a fractional marketing leader. That means I come in as your head of marketing — scoping the GTM strategy, owning the execution, and building the infrastructure that makes growth repeatable. My clients have included companies in robotics, logistics automation, enterprise software, and life sciences.

I work best with Series A and Series B companies that have found product-market fit but haven't yet built the marketing function to match it — and with PE-backed businesses that need to professionalize their go-to-market without hiring a full-time team before they're ready. If that's your situation, I'd like to understand it.
